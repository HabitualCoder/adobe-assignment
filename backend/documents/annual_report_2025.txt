### Annual Report 2025 - Financial Overview
This report provides a summary of our organization's performance during the fiscal year 2025.

#### Revenue Trend
The overall revenue has shown a steady upward trend, increasing by 15% year-over-year. The total revenue for 2025 reached $450M, compared to $390M in 2024. This growth was primarily driven by our expansion into the Asian markets and the launch of our new enterprise SaaS products.

#### Operational Costs
Our operational costs also increased by 10% due to infrastructure scaling and increased hiring in the R&D department.

#### Net Income
The net income for 2025 stands at $120M, representing a healthy profit margin of approximately 26.6%.
