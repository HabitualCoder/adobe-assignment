### Strategic Notes and Risk Assessment

#### Future Strategic Focus
For 2026, the company will focus on integrating Generative AI into its core product suites and enhancing data security protocols.

#### Key Risks Identified
1. **Market Competition**: Increased competition in the mid-market SaaS segment poses a risk to our growth in 2026.
2. **Talent Retention**: As highlighted in the Engineering update, high turnover in technical roles is a critical risk to long-term innovation.
3. **Regulatory Changes**: Evolving data privacy laws in the EU (the upcoming Tech-GDPR 2.0) may require significant system re-architecting.
4. **Supply Chain**: Global hardware shortages may delay our private data center expansion plans.
